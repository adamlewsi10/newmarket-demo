The Challenge

What's Holding Newmarket Back

01

No Single Customer View

Customer interactions are fragmented across Tiger Bay and manual workarounds. No unified record of bookings, enquiries, marketing engagement, complaints, and preferences.

02

Manual Marketing & Comms

Customer communications rely on manual processes. Flight change notifications, upsell campaigns, and post-booking engagement are difficult to execute at scale.

03

Siloed Operational Workflows

Tiger Bay's rigid "categories" and back-office constraints create bottlenecks across cancellations, amendments, and service requests — and can't support the "Flex" model.
